# HERE ARE SOME FORMS THAT I AM AWARE OF BUT YOU CAN DO YOUR RESEARCH

- Deflation: such as of digital storage, intelligence (some podcasts mentioning price of intelligence has fallen down by 40X in the last one year)

- Inflation: costs going up (such as of petrol, medicines, electronics, or anything, etc)

- Stagflation: Salaries have come to a stand still, and prices are going up. So that's stagnation + inflation

- Shrinkflation: Cost of a packet or pouch remaining the same but the quantity in the packet going down. Such as of Parle-G biscuits, or 2-min Maggi noodles.

- Personal Inflation: inflation on actual budget/expenses of an individual
